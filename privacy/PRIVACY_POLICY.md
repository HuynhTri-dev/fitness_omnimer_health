## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following personal details:

**(Collected only with your explicit permission)**

- Blood pressure and resting heart rate

- Workout logs and training metrics

- Device model, OS version
- IP address
- Log data (errors, actions, timestamps)
- Interaction data to improve user experience

### 1.4 Optional Data

## 2. How We Use Your Information

Your data may be used for:

- Providing health insights, analytics, and personalized recommendations
- Syncing and processing health data from supported platforms
- Improving AI models and system performance
- Enhancing user experience and app reliability
- Communicating updates, support responses, or technical notices
- Maintaining security, fraud prevention, and legal compliance

**We do not sell your data.**

---

## 3. How We Store & Protect Data

- All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS.
- Sensitive health data is encrypted at rest.
- Access is strictly permission-controlled based on internal security policies.
- HealthKit data is used only for clearly stated purposes and is **never shared with third parties** for advertising or usage-based data mining.

We do not share your health information with third parties except:

We may share non-personal, aggregated, and anonymized analytics with partners to improve service functionality.
